Introduction of Heroes of the Storm

Click images below to show more information.
Most of you may not hear about this video game before. Heroes of the Storm is a crossover multiplayer online battle arena video game developed and published by Blizzard Entertainment. Unlike traditional MOBA games, Heroes of the Storm emphasizes more on team combat. And players don't need to buy equipments. Instead, each hero has unique talent tree.
Also, almost all heroes in Heroes of the Strom are from other games from Blizzard, including Warcraft, StarCraft, Overwatch and Diablo. There a re some classic heroes like Tracer, Arthas, James Raynor, Jaina and Diablo. And you can find all of them in Heroes of the Storm.
Lastly, we have more than ten different maps and every map has its unique way of playing. Once your team has achieved objectives, your team will gain powerful support (like unleashing the swarm). Or even you can be the powerful monster! So why not join into the fight. Play free now!
