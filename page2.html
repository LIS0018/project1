Experiencing Heroes, Not Occupations

There are over 30 heroes in Overwatch, each of them have unique abilities. Unlike traditional first shooting game or MOBA games, you should understand heroes' strength and weakness so you can get heroes' full capacity. B ut don't worry, you can choose any heroes you like and there is always one for you.

Using Powerful Skills to Control Battlefield

Skills are important in Overwatch. Some heroes can block or prevent enemy's damage; some heroes c an stun or freeze the enemies; some can heal or even revi ve your teammates. What's more, each hero has ultimate skill. Once they are ready, you can use u ltimate skills to crash the enemies!
