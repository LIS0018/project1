Experiencing Heroes, Not Occupations

There are over 30 heroes in Overwatch, each of them have unique abilities. Unlike traditional first shooting game or MOBA games, you should understand heroes' strength and weakness so you can get heroes' full capacity. But don't worry, you can choose any heroes you like and there is always one for you.

Using Powerful Skills to Control Battlefield

Skills are important in Overwatch. Some heroes can block or prevent enemy's damage; some heroes can stun or freeze the enemies; some can heal or even revive your teammates. What's more, each hero has ultimate skill. Once they are ready, you can use ultimate skills to crash the enemies!

Expanding Your Heroes' Collection Gallery

Overwatch has hundreds of heroes' skins. Also, we have much interesting things like voice line, emotions, close-up for you to explore. To expand your collection gallery, you can get loot boxes just by playing quick matches or competitive matches. Or you can buy more loot boxes in the Blizzard store.
